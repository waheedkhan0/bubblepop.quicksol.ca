About Bubble Pop

Bubble Pop is an exciting and addictive bubble shooting puzzle game developed by FireCode Studio. With stunning visuals, smooth gameplay, and hundreds of levels, it offers endless entertainment for players of all ages.

The game combines classic bubble shooter mechanics with modern features like power-ups, daily rewards, and global leaderboards. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive gamer, Bubble Pop has something for everyone!
